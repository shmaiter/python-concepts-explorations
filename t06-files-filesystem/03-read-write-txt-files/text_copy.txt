1.Gather your ingredients and equipment.
2.Whisk dry ingredients.
3.Combine wet ingredients.
4.Make a well in the dry ingredients.
5.Gradually add wet ingredients to dry.
6.Heat waffle iron.
7.Lightly spray the waffle iron.
8.Pour batter onto the waffle iron.
9.Close and cook.
10.Check for doneness.
11.Transfer to a wire rack.
12.Rest your batter.
13.Serve immediately.
14.Clean up.
15.Enjoy!
